How Payment Works

Follow these steps to complete your purchase

1
Redirect to Payment Page

You will be taken to AfriPay's secure checkout page

2
Enter Your MTN Number

Type your MTN Mobile Money phone number (078... or 079...)

3
Receive MTN Payment Prompt

A payment confirmation box will appear on your phone

4
Approve & Pay

Enter your MTN PIN to approve the payment. Done!

Desktop Not Supported

Mobile Money payments only work on a mobile phone. You can browse documents on desktop but cannot pay.
